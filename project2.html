GIET Godavari Institute of Engineering and Technology

Located at Chaitanya Nagar on NH-16 on the outskirts of Rajamahendravaram adjoining the GIET campus, GIET Engineering College is an epitome of excellence in every aspect. Approves by AICTE and affiliated to JNT University Kakinada (JNTUK), the institute offers undergraduate programs in diverse engineering streams. It features state-of-the-art facilities and highly qualified and knowledgeable pool of staff members. Apart from the prescribed course curricula, the institute offers an entire educational experience. It has been a pioneer in adopting several pedagogic innovations for the benefit of students. The achievements of the students of this institution in academics as well as placements stand testimony to the fact.
